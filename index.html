15 days of coding

What is it?

It is a personal goal I set for myself: to build a project every day from the 15th of July to the end of the month. I wanted to push my creativity, bring my wacky ideas to life, and experiment with concepts I learned during Odin's Foundations course. It was fun. It was hectic. (Those spacing bugs? Not it.) But most of all, I thoroughly enjoyed it. This challenge is open to anyone who wants to give it a try. With just the basics you know: set ambitious goals, test your limits — and most importantly, have fun.
